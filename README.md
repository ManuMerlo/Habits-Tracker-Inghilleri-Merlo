## Motivation

In recent years, there's been a noticeable surge in people seeking self-improvement ‚Äî physically, mentally, and emotionally. Yet, one of the prevalent challenges many face is integrating these healthful habits into their daily lives due to a lack of motivation. Our app, **Habits-Tracker**, seeks to address this by not only tracking these activities but transforming them into a game. By introducing game elements like point systems, records, and leaderboards, we've turned the routine task of health tracking into a competitive and enjoyable activity.
